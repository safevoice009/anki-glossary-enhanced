# EnterMedSchool Glossary - Enhanced Edition 🩺⚡

An upgraded, high-yield open-source medical glossary add-on for **Anki Desktop**.

Designed specifically for medical students and graduates preparing for USMLE, PLAB, UPSC CMS, and medical board exams.

## 🌟 Key Features

* **🌐 Universal Double-Click Lookup**: Double-click ANY word or phrase on ANY card to fetch live medical summaries over the internet via the **Wikipedia Medical REST API**.
* **💡 High-Yield Exam Traps & Mnemonics Engine**: Automatically extracts and synthesizes medical board exam traps, mnemonics, precautions, and high-yield pearls.
* **🔤 Smart Suffix & Plural Auto-Matcher**: Automatically matches singular terms to plural forms (`-s`, `-es`) and active/past tenses (`-ed`, `-ing`) on cards.
* **🪟 Movable Desktop Window**: Fully movable, resizable desktop window (`WindowStaysOnTopHint`) that stays pinned on top of Anki while reviewing.
* **📦 564+ Core Offline Terms & 2,695 Patterns**: Built-in lightweight dictionary covering Cardiology, Pulmonology, GI, Nephrology, Endocrine, Heme/Onc, Neuro, Rheum, and Pharmacology.

## 📥 Installation

1. Download `Medical_Terms_Glossary_Instant_Dictionary_Enhanced.zip` from Releases or repo.
2. Open Anki -> **Tools > Add-ons > View Files**.
3. Unzip the files into your Anki add-ons folder.
4. Restart Anki!
